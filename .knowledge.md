### Learned

1. 良好的可扩展性:
    - 扩展性差：CreateData(username, faceURL, status string, message string) (*model.UserFace, error) 
    - 扩展性好：CreateData(Data{Username: username, Status:status, Message: message}) (*model.UserFace, error)
